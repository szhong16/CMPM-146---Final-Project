A project for CMPM146
by Sam Feng, Xufan Huang, Yanwen Xu, Sijia Zhong
We designed it with the goal of encouraging outdoor urban explorations and player interactions.
"world.explore()" will achieve this by providing players with multiple prompt locations every day. Those locations will be publicly and commonly accessible.
(Examples include but are not limited to the Boardwalk, Bell Tower, some restaurants, et cetera.) Players interact by taking a photograph of the prompted location.
Then they can upload the said photograph to world.explore() for our image detection system to determine if the said photograph qualifies to the standards laid out in the prompt.
This is still a prototype.
Hope you like it.
